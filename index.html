F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What industries does ARQ Analytics serve?

    ARQ Analytics primarily serves regulated industries including pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, clinical research, financial services, and healthcare. Our solutions are purpose-built to meet the compliance and auditability demands unique to these sectors.

  • What does "evidence-first AI" mean in practice?

    Evidence-first AI means every model, pipeline, and recommendation we deliver is backed by traceable, documented, and reproducible evidence. Rather than treating AI as a black box, we ensure that every decision point can be explained, audited, and validated — giving regulators and stakeholders full transparency into how conclusions are reached.

  • How do you ensure regulatory compliance in AI models?

    Compliance is built in from day one — not added on top. We align our development process with applicable frameworks (FDA 21 CFR Part 11, ICH E6/E9, GDPR, SOX) and produce validation documentation, model risk assessments, and audit-ready data lineage as standard deliverables. Our pipelines are version-controlled and fully reproducible.

  • Can ARQ integrate with our existing data infrastructure?

    Yes. We are cloud-agnostic and experienced with major data platforms including AWS, Azure, GCP, and on-premise environments. We integrate with existing EDC systems, ERP platforms, data warehouses, and BI tools — minimizing disruption to your current workflows.

  • Do you offer custom AI model development and deployment?

    Absolutely. We develop bespoke machine learning models tailored to your domain and data, with a focus on explainability and validation. We support the full lifecycle — from scoping and feature engineering through model validation, documentation, and production deployment.

  • How do we get started with ARQ Analytics?

    Reach out via our contact form or email us at contact@arq-analytics.com. We typically begin with a discovery call to understand your data landscape, compliance requirements, and business objectives. From there, we propose a tailored engagement — whether a focused audit, a proof-of-concept, or a full-scale implementation.
